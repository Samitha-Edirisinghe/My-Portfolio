Bringing a Media Giant into the Digital : The Redesign of DAN TV

In today's fast-paced digital landscape, a media company's online presence is just as vital as its broadcast reach. This was the driving philosophy behind a recent and exciting project: the complete redesign and development of the official website for DAN TV, a prominent media and entertainment brand under the umbrella of ASK MEDIA.

The goal was clear: to move beyond a simple online brochure and create a dynamic, modern, and intuitive platform that serves as the ultimate digital hub for all things DAN TV. The new website needed to seamlessly integrate news, popular shows, and live broadcasts, all while providing a significantly enhanced user experience.

"In an era where every brand is its own media channel, a media company's website must be more than a passive archive; it must be a living, breathing extension of its broadcast. It is the new front door for audiences who live in a digital-first world. The goal is to not just display content, but to curate an experience that is as compelling and immediate as a live television show, bridging the gap between the screen in the living room and the screen in our hands."

Evelyn Reed, Digital Media Strategist

Beyond the Screen: A Hub for Connection

The new DAN TV website is more than just a place to watch videos; it’s a central hub for the entire DAN TV community. The redesign has transformed the online experience, making it easier for viewers to engage with their favorite content and stay connected with the brand.

Launched on February 22, 2022, the new dantv.lk stands as a testament to the power of thoughtful web design and development. It's a digital reflection of the quality content DAN TV is known for, providing a modern platform that will serve its audience for years to come.

February 22, 2022 Samitha
